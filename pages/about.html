Our Story

FreshCoastal was born in 2020 in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu, with one mission — bring the freshest seafood from the coast directly to your kitchen, with no middleman and no compromise on quality.

Our founder, a third-generation fisherman, noticed families in the city were getting poor-quality, days-old fish at high prices. He set out to fix that by building a direct supply chain from local fishermen to doorsteps.

Today we serve hundreds of families across Tamil Nadu, delivering the morning catch before noon — still fresh, never frozen.
